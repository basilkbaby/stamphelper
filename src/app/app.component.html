Frequently Asked Questions

How many stamps do I need for a letter?

For a standard letter (up to 100g) in the UK, you typically need one 1st class (£1.10) or 2nd class (£0.70) stamp. Use our calculator above for exact requirements.

How do I calculate how many stamps I need?

Our calculator considers three factors: 1) Mail type (letter/parcel), 2) Service (1st/2nd class), and 3) Weight. Enter these details to get your exact stamp requirements.

Can I use multiple stamps if I don't have the right value?

Yes, you can combine stamps to make up the total postage value. Our calculator shows you the exact stamp combination needed.
